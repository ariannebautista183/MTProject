About The Author

Arianne Heart Bautista

Hi! I’m Arianne, the creator of this website. I made this site to showcase the beauty, culture, and pride of my beloved municipality. Through this project, I want to highlight the unique places, traditions, and people that make our community special. My goal is to inspire others to explore and appreciate the charm of our hometown while promoting local tourism and love for our heritage.
